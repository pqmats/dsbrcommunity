# UXBR — Brazilian UI/UX & Design System Community

## Overview

UXBR is a modern tech community platform focused on connecting UI/UX Designers, Product Designers and Design System professionals across Brazil.

The goal of this website is not just to inform, but to feel like a living design movement.

It should feel premium, inspiring and product-driven.

Inspired by:
- designx.community
- linear.app
- luma.com
- modern startup landing pages

---

## Main Goals

- Attract Brazilian designers to join the community
- Promote events and networking
- Showcase Design Systems from Brazilian companies
- Create authority in the Design System ecosystem
- Enable future monetization (jobs, sponsorships, courses)

---

## Target Audience

- UI Designers
- Product Designers
- UX Designers
- Design System specialists
- Junior to Senior designers
- Designers working in tech companies or startups

---

## Brand Personality

- Modern
- Premium
- Inspiring
- Community-driven
- Tech focused
- Educational but not academic
- Friendly but ambitious

---

## Visual Style

- Clean editorial layout
- Strong typography hierarchy
- Large hero section
- Minimal color palette:
  - White background
  - Black primary text
  - Subtle neutral greys
  - Soft gradient accents
- Floating cards
- Soft shadows
- Generous whitespace
- Asymmetric layout sections
- Product storytelling flow

The design must feel like a modern SaaS or startup homepage.

---

## Motion & Interactions

- Scroll reveal animations
- Staggered card entrances
- Hover scale effects
- Depth / elevation transitions
- Subtle parallax elements
- Smooth transitions between sections

Avoid excessive animation. Motion must feel premium and intentional.

---

## Website Structure

### 1. Hero Section

- Very large aspirational headline
- image background with soft gradient accents and floating orbs 
- Supporting subtitle explaining the community
- Social proof stats:
  - Number of designers
  - Number of events
  - Companies involved
- Primary CTA:
  → Join Community
- Secondary CTA:
  → Explore Events
- Optional subtle animated background

---

### 2. Community Section

Explain what UXBR is.

Use short bullet points like:

- Networking between product designers
- Online and in-person events
- Open knowledge sharing
- Career growth opportunities

Include:
- Company logos
- Testimonials from designers

---

### 3. Events Section

- Horizontal scrolling cards
- Upcoming events highlighted
- Past events gallery
- Hover interactions
- Event cards include:
  - Title
  - Date
  - Speaker
  - CTA

---

### 4. Design System Library

Grid of companies that have design systems.

Each card:
- Company logo
- Hover reveal description
- Link to explore

This section is very important for organic traffic and authority.

---

### 5. Job Board Preview

- List of curated UI/UX roles
- Highlighted featured job
- Salary range if possible
- Remote / Hybrid tags

CTA:
→ View all jobs

---

### 6. Talks / Content Section

- Video thumbnails
- Article previews
- Speaker names
- Tags like:
  - Design Systems
  - UX Research
  - Product Strategy

---

### 7. Newsletter / Join Strip

Strong conversion section.

- Gradient background
- Big headline
- Email input
- CTA button

---

### 8. Footer

- Social links
- Community links
- Sponsors area
- Simple navigation

---

## Layout Philosophy

- Sections must breathe
- Avoid dense text blocks
- Prioritize scanning
- Prioritize emotional engagement
- Feels like joining a movement

---

## Future Vision

This platform will evolve into:

- Job marketplace
- Course platform
- Salary research hub
- Certification ecosystem
- Offline conferences

The design should feel scalable and product-ready.
